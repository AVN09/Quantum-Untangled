Are there worlds we are not aware of?

Quantum mechanics is a difficult subject to understand, which has lead to the development of many interpretations of the theory. Let’s take a look at the many-worlds interpretation.

History

The many worlds interpretation of quantum mechanics dates back to 1957, first proposed by Hugh Everett in his Ph.D. thesis titled “The Theory of the Universal Wavefunction”. (This thesis is available here if you are interested on reading it.)
